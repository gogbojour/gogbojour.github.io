About Gogbojour

An empath of the ancestral realm

Mmabatho was called to her gift long before she had the words for it. Today she works as an ancestral empath — reading energy, interpreting signs and symbols through ditaola/intlolo (the throwing of the bones), and translating the vibrational frequency of ko badimong for those who come to her seeking answers.

"S'khulekile ekhaya" — we greet you, from home.

Her readings take place in her Ndumba, the sacred ancestral house where the bones are cast and the ancestors speak, or telephonically for those who cannot reach her in person. Featured as uGogo Emsazakazweni since October 2021, she has become a familiar voice of comfort and clairvoyance to listeners across the country.

◆ A Royal Ndebele Undertaking ◆

The Danalton Ancestral Retreat

In association with Princess Mahlangu of the Ndebele royal house, Gogbojour is building an ancestral retreat at Danalton, eNtwana, near Siyabuswa — a homestead for healing, ceremony and return to self.

Reference image of a traditional Ndebele homestead layout — main house, principal Ndumba, guest Ndumbas and family houses around a painted courtyard. Danalton's actual build photos coming as construction progresses.

Where

Danalton, eNtwana, near Siyabuswa — seat of the Ndzundza-Mabhoko Ndebele royal house, a region long known for keeping Ndebele heritage, beadwork and tradition alive across generations.

What's Being Built

A main house, a principal Ndumba for ceremony and readings, several guest Ndumbas, and family houses set within the homestead yard — a permanent home for healing, not a one-time event.
